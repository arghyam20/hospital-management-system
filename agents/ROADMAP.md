# Roadmap

## Q1: Core Foundation & Patient Portal
- Complete Phase 1 and 2 of implementation.
- Launch MVP allowing Patients to register, view profiles, and browse Doctor specialties.

## Q2: Clinical Operations
- Roll out Appointment Booking, Pharmacy, and Lab integrations.
- Doctors can manage schedules and write digital prescriptions.

## Q3: Financials & Auditing
- Enable full Billing flow with automated invoice generation.
- Enable Audit system for compliance (HIPAA).
- Integrate third-party payment gateways (Stripe).

## Q4: Advanced Features & Mobile App
- Launch mobile application (React Native) utilizing the same API Gateway.
- Implement advanced analytics dashboards for Hospital Administrators.
- Integrate Telemedicine (Video Consultation) directly within the Appointments module.
